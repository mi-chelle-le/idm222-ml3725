Date

Tools

About

A lighthearted motion graphic animation that takes us through the struggles of attempting to create art, and feeling like the universe is doing everything in its power to stop us. Each scene consists of varying complementary colors. All parts were drawn on Procreate, then taken into Adobe After Effects to animate.
